Install Salad House Rewards

To use this app, you need to add it to your home screen first. It only takes a few seconds!

How to install
1
Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari
2
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3
Tap "Add" in the top right, then open from your home screen
1
Tap the three dots menu in the top right of Chrome
2
Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home screen"
3
Tap "Install" and open from your home screen
