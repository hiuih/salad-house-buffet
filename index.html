A Fresh Take on Healthy Eating

At Salad House Buffet, we believe eating healthy should never mean compromising on taste. Nestled in the heart of City Square, Vancouver, we bring you a vibrant buffet experience with a clean salad bar, an incredible selection of food, and a great variety of fresh vegetables and fruits.

Every bowl, every plate, every bite is prepared with super fresh ingredients and served with the friendliest service in town.
